About World Malayalee Council

Welcome to the International Health and Medical Forum, an initiative brought to you by the International Tourism Forum of the World Malayalee Council. We are dedicated to revolutionizing the health and medical tourism experience for expatriate Malayalees and individuals worldwide. Our mission is to provide a seamless, transparent, trustworthy, and efficient platform for all your health and medical tourism needs.
